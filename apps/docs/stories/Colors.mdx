## Non-negotiables

- Main Blue is **only** for accents, strokes, and CTAs — never a large background.
- Headings use **Navy** (`#161643`) in-product or **Dark** (`#19194B`) in marketing. **Never pure black**.
- Empty inputs are `#F4F6F8` (gray-tinted). Filled inputs gain a `#161643` 1px border to signal they have content.
- Shadows are blue-tinted (`rgba(26,148,255, …)`) — never pure black shadows in-product.
